About Me

Hello! My name is Kirk Trachy and I am a software sales engineer with Quick Base, Inc.

Quick Base is a platform as a service, (PaaS) offering much like an online web database. It provides rapid application development of any business application like, project management, crm, content management, learning management and in general anything you can say outloud.

I've worked at Quick Base since 2005 and find that working at Quick Base is the most fun I've ever had.

I hold six live webinars each week and all are welcome to attend. We talk about how to use and customize Quick Base into any type of application use case.
